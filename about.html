About Me

Academic Interests

My primary research interests lie in:

  • Computational Physics: Developing numerical methods and simulations for complex physical systems
  • Theoretical Physics: Mathematical frameworks and theoretical models in modern physics

Skills & Expertise

Throughout my academic career, I have developed proficiency in:

  • Programming languages: Python, C++, Matlab
  • Scientific computing and data analysis
  • Mathematical modeling and simulation
  • LaTeX for scientific documentation

Personal Philosophy

I believe in the power of interdisciplinary approaches to solve complex problems in physics. By combining theoretical understanding with computational tools, I strive to contribute to advancing our knowledge of the physical world.
